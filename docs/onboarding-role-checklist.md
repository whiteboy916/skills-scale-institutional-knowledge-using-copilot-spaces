# Onboarding Role Checklist

Use this checklist when onboarding new team members to help them understand their role, responsibilities, and key interactions within the OctoAcme project management framework.

---

## For All Roles

- [ ] Read `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` to understand all team members' roles
- [ ] Review `docs/role-interaction-matrix.md` to see how your role interacts with others
- [ ] Understand your primary responsibilities and goals
- [ ] Identify your key stakeholders and communication patterns
- [ ] Get access to required tools and systems (e.g., project management tool, code repositories, communication channels)
- [ ] Schedule introductions with team members you'll collaborate with most
- [ ] Review current project status and recent decisions (check project board, decision logs, meeting notes)
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and how to raise issues or blockers
- [ ] Complete relevant training or onboarding sessions for your role

---

## For Product Managers

- [ ] Review current product strategy and roadmap
- [ ] Understand customer segments and use cases
- [ ] Review product success metrics and KPIs
- [ ] Meet with Stakeholder Representatives to understand customer needs
- [ ] Review existing product documentation and specifications
- [ ] Understand prioritization framework and how decisions are made
- [ ] Set up regular sync meetings with Project Manager and Technical Lead
- [ ] Review recent customer feedback and feature requests
- [ ] Understand financial constraints and budget for current initiatives

---

## For Project Managers

- [ ] Review current project plans and timelines
- [ ] Understand project scope, constraints, and success criteria
- [ ] Meet with all key personas to understand their roles
- [ ] Review risk register and mitigation strategies
- [ ] Understand project governance and decision-making authority
- [ ] Set up communication cadence with stakeholders
- [ ] Review project documentation and historical lessons learned
- [ ] Understand resource allocation and team capacity
- [ ] Set up weekly status review process with team leads
- [ ] Understand how to escalate issues and when to involve Risk Manager

---

## For Developers

- [ ] Review current codebase and development standards
- [ ] Set up development environment and verify build process
- [ ] Understand current sprint and upcoming work
- [ ] Meet with Technical Architect to understand architecture and standards
- [ ] Review coding guidelines and best practices
- [ ] Understand testing requirements and coverage expectations
- [ ] Learn how to work with QA Lead on quality standards
- [ ] Understand deployment process and Release Manager's role
- [ ] Review recent design decisions and technical architecture docs
- [ ] Set up daily standup attendance and communication channels

---

## For QA Leads

- [ ] Review testing strategy and quality standards
- [ ] Understand acceptance criteria framework
- [ ] Meet with Developers to align on quality requirements
- [ ] Review test automation framework and tools
- [ ] Understand defect management process and severity levels
- [ ] Review quality metrics and reporting dashboards
- [ ] Meet with Release Manager to understand release readiness criteria
- [ ] Understand when manual vs. automated testing is appropriate
- [ ] Review performance and security testing requirements
- [ ] Understand escalation process for critical quality issues

---

## For Release Managers

- [ ] Review deployment processes and infrastructure
- [ ] Understand release schedule and cadence
- [ ] Meet with DevOps/Infrastructure team
- [ ] Review rollback procedures and disaster recovery plans
- [ ] Understand communication process for deployments
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand deployment requirements
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to understand quality sign-off process
- [ ] Review deployment checklists and gates
- [ ] Understand stakeholder communication requirements
- [ ] Set up deployment coordination meetings and on-call processes

---

## For Technical Architects

- [ ] Review technical architecture and design standards
- [ ] Understand current technology stack and constraints
- [ ] Review architectural decision records and historical decisions
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand technical challenges
- [ ] Understand scalability and performance requirements
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand long-term technical vision
- [ ] Review security and compliance requirements
- [ ] Understand how to support Developers through design reviews
- [ ] Review technical debt and improvement initiatives
- [ ] Establish regular architecture review cadence

---

## For Stakeholder Representatives

- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand product strategy
- [ ] Review customer segments and user personas
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand current initiatives
- [ ] Understand stakeholder communication preferences and cadence
- [ ] Review customer feedback and feature requests
- [ ] Understand decision-making authority and escalation paths
- [ ] Set up regular communication with your stakeholder group
- [ ] Understand how to provide feedback from stakeholders to the team
- [ ] Review recent project demos and releases
- [ ] Establish trust with team members through 1:1 meetings

---

## For Documentation Specialists

- [ ] Review documentation standards and templates
- [ ] Understand current documentation structure and repository
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand product documentation needs
- [ ] Meet with Technical Architect to understand technical documentation needs
- [ ] Review existing process documentation
- [ ] Understand versioning and documentation maintenance process
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand how they contribute to documentation
- [ ] Review user guide and training materials
- [ ] Understand accessibility and usability requirements for documentation
- [ ] Set up regular documentation review meetings with stakeholders

---

## For Risk Managers

- [ ] Review current risk register and mitigation strategies
- [ ] Understand organizational risk tolerance and thresholds
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand project-specific risks
- [ ] Review risk assessment framework and methodology
- [ ] Meet with Technical Architect to understand technical risks
- [ ] Understand escalation process for high-impact risks
- [ ] Review insurance and compliance requirements
- [ ] Set up regular risk assessment meetings
- [ ] Meet with all team leads to gather risk insights
- [ ] Establish communication cadence for risk reporting

---

## Completion Steps

- [ ] Complete all applicable role-specific checklist items above
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with at least 3-4 key collaborators from the matrix
- [ ] Attend your first full team meeting
- [ ] Review and sign off on understanding of role and responsibilities
- [ ] Get manager/team lead sign-off on onboarding completion
- [ ] Set follow-up check-in for 2 weeks and 1 month after start date

## Notes

- The onboarding period typically takes 2-4 weeks to complete fully
- Don't hesitate to ask questions or request clarification during onboarding
- After 1 month, schedule a feedback session to discuss how onboarding went
